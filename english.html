IT'S OFFICIAL: MEN ARE FINALLY
DARING TO WEAR SKIRTS
AND THEY ARE PROUD OF IT!

Is the skirt becoming
a staple in
men's wardrobes?

One might wonder why it took so long for the skirt to be accepted (or at least tolerated) in men's wardrobes.

Let's take a look at what's happening around the world. In many countries, there are numerous garments worn by men that can be compared to skirts and dresses: the kanga, a kind of wrap similar to the sarong in sub-Saharan Africa, the fustanella (a short, flared cotton skirt) in Greece and the Balkans, or the lava-lava in the Pacific.

It's time to dare like Brad Pitt.
